My Group's Game Proposal

Grace Adeboyejo / August 2,2021

On Friday, July 30,2021, My group created a proposal for a game that would help teachers create more inclusive spaces for gender identity.

1. Our game was based off of the game 'Among Us'. In Among Us, you have tasks you have to complete as a crewmemeber. So, we decided that the teachers would have a task to remember the pronouns of all of their students.

2. In Amoung Us, there are different maps you can play on. Our map was a classroom.

3. The avatars in our game are randomly assigned so that teachers could get used to seeing different combinations of "students". This means that the pronouns did not match up with the avatar so the teacher could remember the student's pronouns from memory and not based on what the avatar looked like.
